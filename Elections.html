1991 Parliamentary Elections

The Fifth National Parliamentary Elections were held on 27 February 1991. The Bangladesh Nationalist Party won 140 of 300 seats. Voter turnout was 55%

February 1996 Parliamentary Elections

The Sixth National Parliamentary Elections were held on 15 February 1996. Most opposition parties boycotted the elections. The Bangladesh National Party won all 300 seats. Voter turnout was 21%.

June 1996 Parliamentary Elections

The Seventh National Parliamentary Elections were held on 12 June 1996. The Awami League won 146 of 300 seats. Voter turnout was 76%

2001 Parliamentary Elections

The Eighth National Parliamentary Elections were held on 1 October 2001. The Bangladesh Nationalist Party-backed Four Party Alliance won 193 of 300 seats. Voter turnout was 75%

2008 Parliamentary Elections

The Ninth National Parliamentary Elections were held on 29 December 2008. The Awami League-backed Mohajote coalition won 262 of 300 seats. Voter turnout was 80%

2014 Parliamentary Elections
