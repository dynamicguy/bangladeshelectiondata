2008 Parliamentary Elections | Political Party Margin of Victory by Electoral Constituency

This map highlights the margins of victory of political parties by electoral constituency. The Mohajote coalition, anchored by the Awami League, won 262 of 300 seats. The average margin of victory for seats won by Mohajote coalition members was 21%; the median was 19%. The success of the Mohajote coalition represented a 200+ seat gain for the Awami League and its partners.



December 2008 Parliamentary Elections
